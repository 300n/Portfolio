How We Collect

Behind
Every
Work

Every piece in the FORMA collection goes through a rigorous, artist-centred acquisition process. We prioritise integrity over speculation.

Work With Us
Our Process
01
Artist Nomination
Artists are nominated by our curatorial board, peer artists, or through open submissions each quarter.
02
Studio Review
Our curators visit the artist's studio, understanding their practice, context, and trajectory before any acquisition.
03
Ethical Pricing
We guarantee fair, transparent pricing negotiated directly with the artist. No speculation. No flipping.
04
Placement & Provenance
Every work is placed in collections or exhibitions that honour the artist's intent and long-term legacy.
